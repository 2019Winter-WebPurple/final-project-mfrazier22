Biography

      Ruchi Sanghvi is an Indian engineer who has worked for major companies such as Facebook and Dropbox. She is married to another engineer and coworkers Aditya Agarwal. They have worked on projects together.

Cove and Dropbox

     Ruchi Sanghvi was the VP of Operations at Dropbox. Prior to joining Dropbox, Sanghvi served as the co-founder and CEO of Cove, a collaboration, coordination and communication product for organizations and communities. Dropbox later bought Cove to bring Ruchi and her husband back to work for them. When asking Dropbox co-founder and CEO Drew Huston about the decision to buy Cove to obtain Ruchi Sanghvi, he said “We’ve known Ruchi and Aditya for awhile and have been longtime admirers of their work, there are not too many people that not only think big but have also created things that hundreds of million of people use.” More parts of the interview with Drew Huston about Ruchi Sanghvi can be found here.

Facebook

     Sanghvi holds the distinction of being the first female engineer at Facebook and was instrumental in implementing the first versions of key features such as News Feed. This caused many problems regarding the privacy of Facebook users, which caused the Facebook engineering team to work for 48 straight hours in order to fix the addressed problems. She then led product management and strategy for Facebook Platform and Facebook Connect. She was also responsible for core product areas such as privacy and user engagement.

Why She Is She Influential?

     She is an influentual woman who has the ability to inspire others to enter the field of engineering, create their own company, or become one of the only females in a male-dominated career. She was interviewed by Huffington Post regarding the challenges of being a female engineer. More information can be found about Ruchi's story here.
